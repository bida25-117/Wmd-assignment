About Us

Building Botswana's premier competitive gaming ecosystem

Who We Are

31 Esports is Botswana’s leading esports organization, founded in 2023, dedicated to developing competitive gaming talent.

Our Mission

To discover, develop, and elevate gaming talent in Botswana by creating competitive opportunities and professional pathways for aspiring esports players.

Our Vision

To grow competitive gaming across Botswana and Africa, helping players earn, gain recognition, and reach their full potential on a global stage.
